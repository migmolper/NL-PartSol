Function to compute is a material point is or not eroded. Here the notation is the same as in the paper : A.Pandolfi & M.Ortiz. An eigenerosion approach to brittle fracture. International Journal for Numerical Methods in Enginnering. 92:694-714, 2012.

Inputs :

Parameters
p: Index of the particle
ji: Damage status
W: Internal work
Mass: Mass field
Rho: Density field
Stress: Stress field of each particle
Properties: Define the material properties of the particle
B_eps: Define the particles close to each particle
DeltaX: Mesh size
